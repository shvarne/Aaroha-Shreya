Donate Now

Let's donate to needy people for better lives

Aaroha is serving the underprivileged children since its birth and to get it all going smoothly for those brilliant children, we are introducing a One Day One Rupee programme for the society. You can easily spend a lot of money on trips and food on weekends and birthdays, but all that money becomes memory after that momentarily pleasure. You can give us and yourself as well that constant motivation of living life in an extraordinary way of helping others by donating us at least 1 rupee in a day.... The total amount would be 30 in a month and 360 in a year which is of course minimal and worth doing. Those who aspire to work for the underprivileged section of the society but due to busy schedule and the prevailing rush in their life they are unable to give time have a golden opportunity to serve the innocent children through this challenge. Quoting Mahatma Gandhi's words, “Be the change you wish to see in the world” we believe that we would get an overwhelming response from the society we work for.
